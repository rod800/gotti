About

The popularity of Bully dogs as pets to lovers is increasing, but they are still uncommon or is some cases rare in terms of rarity. The main reason for this is that many breeders did not breed tricolored dogs for several generations since they can be mistaken as mixed breeds and mixed breed Bullies are something breeders try to avoid producing. The caution of the breeders is understandable since a pure-breed Bully dog with a distinguished pedigree is one of the many traits valued by any potential buyer. As a result, the Bully gene pool rarely produces tri-colored dogs even when breeders try to breed them purposely into existence.
